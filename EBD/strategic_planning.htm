Strategic Planning

It is essential before sale or purchase sufficient time and thought is dedicated to strategic planning, to achieve the best possible result.

  • Determine objective for the purchase/sale

  • Identify marketing approach.

  • Agree routes for research 

  • Identify potential Vendors/Purchasers

  • Consider funding opportunities if appropriate

  • Consider professional representation

  • Agree production of  profile and prospectus

  • Prioritise approach

  • Appoint  European Business Development
