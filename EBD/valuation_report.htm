Valuation Report

On receipt of appropriate financial and market information. European Business Development will provide a valuation report relating to the vendors business, referring to the following.

  • The Company

  • Fiscal & Trading Styles

  • Financial Data & Indicators

  • Contract Sales & Contribution

  • Market Overview

  • Exit Formula's

  • Valuation Range(s)

  • Methods of Payment

  • Fair Value Assessment

  • Goodwill Valuation

  • Methods and Structures of Payment
