RAISING FINANCE TO SUPPORT THE ACQUISITION

 

External funding to support an acquisition may comprise some or all of the following:  

  • Bank loans and overdrafts - conventional lending secured on the assets of the company

  • Asset finance - lending against specific assets, eg property, plant & machinery, debtors, stock

  • Acquisition/Mezzanine finance - lending based upon an established track record, often where security is lacking

  • Equity Finance - "risk capital" provided by venture capital companies

European Business Development can advise on the funding structure appropriate for the transaction and organise the raising of finance through its established relationships with venture capital providers, banks and asset based lenders.
