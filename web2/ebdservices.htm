Glossary of Terms

 

 

Marketing, Valuation, Acquisition & Merger 

European Business Development provides clients, both purchasers and vendors with confidential advice relating to marketing, valuation, acquisition and merger of company assets or services.  We tailor our services to satisfy the needs of individual clients during this sensitive and confidential period.  European Business Development provides the latest market and fiscal information to help clients to make rational and realistic decisions to suit their own position.  European Business Development service to all parties are strictly confidential.

Mission Statement

Protect the confidentiality of the client company from other companies, time wasters, and unscrupulous people within the market place and company employee’s. Following a realistic assessment of the client company’s financial trading performance and its perceived position within its market place.  Meet genuine vendors or buyers, who after a full briefing will clearly understand our client needs and terms and conditions of business.

Marketing Services

Advise a positive marketing strategy to assist the purchaser or vendor to reach a favourable decision to buy or sell.  This provides both with a list of potential companies whom meet client requirements relating to merger, purchase or sell.  Services include market analysis, investigation and submission through to “Heads of Agreement” and “Exchange of Contract”.

Valuation Report

On receipt of the appropriate financial and market information, European Business Development will provide a valuation on the vendors business, comparing current market prices to the prescribed asking purchase price.

Fees

Our fees for providing Valuation Report and Marketing Services are payable upon appointment.  Conducting marketing assignments outside our standard terms is negotiable to account for specific client requirements.

Terms & Conditions 

Exchanged before commencement of assignment to ensure good working practice on all sides

Observation

The business of buying and selling a business is at times a sensitive and emotional occasion.  Buying and selling near to the expectations of the client company requires careful strategic planning and judgement.  On the question of confidentiality negotiations are best kept outside the purchasers and vendors offices to avoid indiscretions.
