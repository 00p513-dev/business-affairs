Acquisition Or Merger Services

European Business Development will be pleased to help client companies who wish to develop existing business through acquisition or merger.

European Business Development will also on request provide advice on how best to raise the necessary funding to satisfy merger or purchasing proposals.

We are also in a position to offer a full team of professional advisors who will protect your best interests throughout negotiations.

Heads Of Terms

European Business Development will on the instructions of clients prepare "Heads of Terms" for signing off subject to contract by both parties.
