Marketing & Valuation

Marketing Services

We advise a positive marketing strategy to assist the vendor or purchaser to reach a favourable decision to sell or buy. We provide through advertising or interrogation of our data files a list of potential purchasers or vendor companies who meet the requirements relating to sale, merger or purchase.

Services include;

  • Market analysis.

  • Financial data and key indicators.

  • Profit & Loss trading report.

  • Fair Value.

  • Information pack.

On instructions from the client, we will also draw and sign both parties to a Heads of Terms Agreement, subject to contract.

 

Valuation Report

European Business Development is requested from time to time to provide a valuation on a vendors business. On receipt of the appropriate financial and market information, European Business Development will provide a valuation relating to the trading conditions and results of the vendors business.

We take into account;

  • Actual and historical fiscal performance of the company.

  • Position within the market place.

  • Management style and integrity.

  • Risk & reward values.

  • Fiscal Add Backs.

  • Fair values to suit specific buyers.

  • Trade (stand alone)

  • Trade (asset strip)

  • Outside buyer.

The client is required to provide fiscal, trading and marketing information on request to allow European to provide a bound confidential report.
