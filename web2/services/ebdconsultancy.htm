Consultancy Services  

European Business Development has a wealth of experience in providing practical support and advice to owner-managed companies, aimed at assisting them with most stages of their growth and development. As well as providing high quality consultancy advice, we are available to assist with the vitally important implementation process.

We work closely with clients and provide a comprehensive range of services including:  

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

  • Business Planning - assistance in formulating policies, objectives, strategies and action plans, incorporating these into a strategic business plan.

  • Marketing - formulation of detailed marketing plans covering all aspects of the marketing mix.

  • Change Management - development and implementation of rescue, turnaround, profit improvement and cost reduction programmes.

  • Team Building - review and assessment of organisational issues, assistance in developing organisation structures and management teams.

  • Pre-Sale Planning - development of exit strategies and implementation of measures to ensure that, at the time of sale, a company is in the best possible shape.  

MANAGEMENT SERVICES

  • Ongoing Support and Advice - ongoing support, general management advice, non-executive director and company doctor type roles.

  • Recruitment Services - assistance with the search, selection and recruitment of key personnel.

  • Investigations and Reviews – assistance with the evaluation of new ventures, expansion plans and capital expenditure projects etc.

FINANCIAL CONTROLS

  • Budgetary Controls - preparation of detailed monthly profit & loss account and balance sheet budgets to evaluate profit potential and provide a basis for monitoring future performance.

  • Cash Flow Projections – compilation of cash forecasts to determine future funding requirements and facilitate effective financial controls.

  • Corporate Finance – advice on restructuring existing borrowings and raising finance to provide additional working capital or to support expansion plans.

  • Management Accounts – preparation of monthly or quarterly management accounts together with variance investigation and reporting.

  • Systems and Procedures - design and implementation of computer based accounting and enterprise systems.
