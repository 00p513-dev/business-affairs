Acquisition Support Services  

In addition to providing acquisition search services, European Business Development often act as lead advisers in the transaction process, providing a range of additional services as required.  

Business Plan/Funding proposals

Essential to the acquisition process, is the preparation of a detailed strategic business plan, particularly where external funding is required to support the acquisition. As well as reviewing the existing business, the plan should focus on the rationale behind the proposed acquisition, market prospects and post acquisition strategies. Detailed monthly financial projections should evaluate future profit potential and incorporate the proposed funding structure, demonstrating the ability to service borrowings, both interest and capital. Through its consultancy expertise, European Business Development and associates has extensive experience in the preparation of strategic business plans and funding proposals.
