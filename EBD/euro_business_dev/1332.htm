Key Facts

Management team and employees

The shareholders/directors oversee the running of the business, however it is actively managed by the recruitment, financial and office manager on a day to day basic. The Company has 10 full time employees, which includes 6 recruitment consultants but excludes the directors.

Services

The Company’s principle activity is the recruitment of social workers.

Customers

The Company has 50 – 60 active customers that they supply qualified social workers to on a regular basis. The customer base represents the NHS trust and local authorities located primarily in the South East of England.

Historical Strategy

Organic growth by reinvesting the profits generated in the business. The ability of the Company to be able to supply social workers to customers on "demand" led to the exponential growth in revenue.

Financials

Consistently profitable business. Highly cash generative.

Background and Future Prospects

The Company was founded by its shareholders in 2001 to take advantage of the national shortage of skilled social workers where current demand by the NHS, local authorities and county councils exceeds supply. The Company is a specialist staffing agency in the placement of social workers predominantly in the South East of England.

The Company operates from premises located in the South East, however the business could be based anywhere in the UK.

The shareholders have grown revenue and adjusted earnings before tax to approximately £2.6 million and £324,000 respectively in the financial year to 30 April 2003. The Company’s adjusted earnings before tax for the 7 months to 30 November 2003 is approximately £290,000.

The Company since incorporation is developing into a significant player in this field with an impeccable reputation. It has built-up niche sector knowledge in the industry, which has contributed to its successful organic growth in revenue from a zero base to £1.4 million in its first year of operation and a further 80% increase in its second year. This is a result of following a strategy of identifying, sourcing and placing the right applicants on "demand" to its customers. This success is also largely attributable to the experience and dedication of the existing management team in place.

Having grown the Company to its current size over the last 3 years and effectively implemented a strong management structure in the business. The shareholders now wish to sell the Company and return home to the United States. There are some strong commercial reasons that a trade sale would be the preferred route. The shareholders are willing to remain involved for an agreed period of time to effect a smooth transition for the new owners.

Future growth prospects are excellent with the social care market expected to experience a continued shortage of qualified social workers and is anticipated to increase. The Company expect revenue to increase conservatively at approximately 25% per annum for at least the next 3 years. There is also further scope to grown revenue by expanding the geographical base of its customers and with the expected appointment to process work visas.

Services and Customers

The Company specialises in the provision of quality temporary and permanent qualified social works to the NHS and other local government departments (local authorities) that are located primarily in the South East and the Midlands.

The Company recruits candidates both locally and offshore. When social workers are recruited they work exclusively for the Company on a temporary contract with a client for generally six months after which they have the option to be placed permanently or continue with the Company on a temporary basis.

The Company has developed very good relationships with its customers. This strong relationship is indicative of the majority of sales coming from repeat customers.

The Company has received numerous requests from other government departments to supply them with social workers. However, the Company has intentionally turned away any new business recently as it prefers to maintain the high level of service offered to its existing customer base.
