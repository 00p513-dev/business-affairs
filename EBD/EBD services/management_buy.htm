Management Buy-Outs/Buy-Ins  

European Business Development provide a personal service to clients, with a high level of commitment and support, often managing the transaction process from concept through to completion. This approach is particularly suited to Management Buy-Out (MBO) and Management Buy-In (MBI) transactions where clients have little or no previous experience on which to draw.  

Through our Acquisition Search Service we can help MBI clients locate target companies.


From our experience in selling companies for clients, we are well placed to advise on company valuations.

Through our Acquisition Support Services, we can compile business plans/funding proposals, advise on funding structures, raise finance and manage the progress of the transaction through each stage.


Our fee structure for advising on MBO and MBI transactions is competitive, agreed in advance of our accepting an appointment, and largely success related.


Following completion of an acquisition, we are on hand, if required, to provide a range of specialist Consultancy Services, aimed at assisting clients achieve their objectives and performance expectations.
