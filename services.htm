Services

Established in 1989 Business Affairs operates predominantly in building/industry support services providing an extensive range of recruitment and HR services to our clients.

Recruitment Services

File Search: Working from a extensive data base created over time spent operating in this sector we can screen candidates against very specific criteria

Direct Search: Industry experienced consultants who source candidates against clients specifications

Advertised Assignment: Developing copy and placing it within National/Regional press or industry journals, Screening and interviewing candidates and producing a final shortlist for the client .

 

HR Services

Profiling: For many of our clients we carry out psychometric/personality profiling of existing and potential personnel

Outplacement: Counselling individuals on how to conduct themselves when entering the job market and opportunities which may be available to them

Appraisal/Evaluation: Meeting with key personnel within a clients organisation and offering an independent assessment of an individuals compatibility against their job description.

Remuneration Surveys: Collecting data relevant to industry sector and position as requested by the client

 

Many other services are provided and customised dependant upon the clients specification.
