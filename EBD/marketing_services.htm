Marketing Services        

European Business Development provides clients, both purchasers and vendors with confidential advice relating to marketing, valuation, acquisition and merger of company’s or services.  We tailor our services to satisfy the needs of individual clients during this sensitive and confidential period.  European Business Development provides the latest market and fiscal information to help clients to make rational and realistic decisions to sit there own position.  European Business Development services to all parties are strictly confidential.

We advise a positive marketing strategy to assist the purchaser to reach a favourable decision.  This provides the client with a list of potential companies who meet their requirements. Services include market analysis, investigation and submission through to “Heads of Agreement” and “Exchange of Contract”.

Marketing Strategy

Following the Strategic Planning Meeting and client briefing. European Business Development will propose a marketing strategy to suit the client's specific needs.

The business of buying and selling a business is at times a sensitive and emotional occasion.  Buying and selling near to the expectations of the Client Company requires careful strategic planning and judgement.  On the question of confidentiality negotiations are best kept outside the purchasers and vendors offices to avoid indiscretions.  Many a slip between cup and lip in this incestuous industry.

Our services are available to clients through out the UK and Europe.
