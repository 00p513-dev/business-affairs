Marketing Services

We agree with clients a favourable marketing strategy (rifle or shotgun) to discreetly notify the marketplace of clients interest. This provides the client with a selection of potential vendors who may wish to sell. Vendors will normally at this stage provide a report relating to the fiscal and trading style of the vendor company.

Services include copy writing, advertising, responding and meeting with genuine vendors on exchange of confidentially agreement.

Our modern offices are available for initial meetings with prospective purchases.

Marketing Strategy

Following the Strategic Planning Meeting and client briefing. European Business Development will propose a marketing strategy to suit the client's specific needs.

Acquisition Or Merger Services

European Business Development will be pleased to help client companies who wish to develop existing business through acquisition or merger.

European Business Development will also on request provide advice on how to best raise the necessary funding to satisfy merger or purchase proposals.

We are also in a position to offer a full team of professional advisors to assist with funding, accountancy, due diligence and legal representation throughout negotiations.
