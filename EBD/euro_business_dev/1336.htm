Summary of Company

The Company was formed in 1999 and services a number of clients in the West London area with the main clients all being based in a single multi tenanted building, where its agreement with the landlord includes the day to day facilities management of the building.

The company’s primarily offers two services, which are maintenance & cleaning. The maintenance works are carried out either by one of the company’s full time staff or by subcontractors depending on the actual tasks. The cleaning is carried out by a team or part cleaners and managed by a part time cleaning manager.

The one off engineering works are carried out for a range of clients.

The company’s turnover is as follows:

Regular Maintenance £155,000

Regular Cleaning £140,000

One off Engineering £193,000

Consumable Sales £ 23,000

Total £511,000

 

In addition to the part time cleaning labour force the company employs 6 full time staff as follows:

 

General Manager

Secretary

Part Time Cleaning Supervisor

3 Maintenance People

Only the maintenance personnel are considered essential for continued operations.

The company owns one van. It has no mobile phones or company cars and staff are paid on a mileage basis.

Either the customer base or the share capital can be purchased.
