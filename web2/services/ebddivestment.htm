Divestment Services   

Selling or buying a company is the most important event in your business life. You will probably only buy or sell your company once, and it is vital that you maximise your opportunity. European Business Development provide a dedicated, proactive and confidential marketing service, managing the sale process from concept through to completion, leaving you free to concentrate on the management of your company.  

We proactively market your company through:

  • Our database of acquisitive companies and individuals

  • Our extensive network of contacts with solicitors, accountants, banks and professionals engaged in corporate acquisitions and disposals

  • Dedicated sector research to search out potential purchasers

  • Direct approaches to targeted acquirers

  • Discreet, anonymous advertising in national press, specialist publications and on-line directories

The service is professional, personal and confidential, our activities and approach aimed at realising maximum sales value for shareholders.  


With this clear objective in mind, we manage the sale process, our service including:

  • Consideration of shareholder options, aspirations and objectives

  • Advice on company valuation

  • Agreement of a sale timetable and action plan

  • Preparation of a detailed Information Memorandum, describing the company, its financial performance and future prospects

  • Proactive, confidential marketing

  • Enquiry management, issue of confidentiality documentation

  • Negotiations with interested parties, consideration of offers, advice on deal structuring

  • Liaison with other professional advisers (accountants, solicitors, due diligence teams)

  • Co-ordination of the sale process through to completion  

Our fee structure is competitive and agreed in advance of our accepting an appointment. With the exception of a fee for our work in Marketing, producing the Information Memorandum or Valuation Report, our fees are success related, based on an agreed percentage of the sale consideration - no sale, no fee, unless mandated by the vendor.

The initial meeting to discuss shareholder options and aspirations is confidential, free and without obligation.
