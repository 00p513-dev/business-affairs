Acquisition Search Services  

As specialist brokers, European Business Development continuously network with accountants, solicitors, corporate finance firms, banks, venture capital companies, and professionals engaged in corporate acquisitions and disposals. Through these contacts and subscription to specialist publications and on-line directories, we receive early notification of companies that are for sale, considering a sale, or "open to offers", and are therefore well placed to provide acquisition search services for clients.  


We initially meet with clients, without obligation, to discuss their business, its future plans and objectives. We then consider acquisition criteria and compile a target company profile.


For clients actively seeking acquisitions, we offer a dedicated search service where we are retained to proactively locate target acquisitions. We undertake market sector research to determine companies broadly meeting a client's acquisition criteria. We organise mail shots, make discreet approaches, identify vendors who would be receptive to an approach, arrange confidentiality documentation, and organise exploratory meetings.


Alternatively, for clients with a general interest in acquisitions, and no fixed timescales, we are happy to enter their details onto our database free of charge, and they will be supplied with details of appropriate companies for sale as they are received by us.


Where clients are interested in a merger or strategic alliance rather than an outright sale or purchase, we provide search services to locate potential opportunities.


Our fee structure is competitive and agreed in advance of our accepting an appointment. With the exception of a commitment fee already stated where we undertake dedicated acquisition or merger searches for clients, our fees are success related, payable on completion of the transaction – no purchase, no fee.


Once an acquisition target had been identified and a deal agreed, we can provide a comprehensive range of services, or we are happy to work with existing advisers.
